# Scheme Data Source Notes

Member 4 should keep this file updated whenever new schemes are added to `schemes.csv`.

## Sources Used For Added Schemes

- PM Ujjwala Yojana: official PMUY portal, eligibility says adult woman from poor household with no existing LPG connection in the household. Source: https://www.pmuy.gov.in/
- PM Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana: Department of Food and Public Distribution page describes free food grains for eligible beneficiaries. Source: https://dfpd.gov.in/pradhan-mantri-garib-kalyan-anna-yojana/en
- Sukanya Samriddhi Account: India Post small savings page for girl child savings account. Source: https://www.indiapost.gov.in/VAS/pages/pmodashboard/sukanyasamriddhiaccount.aspx
- PM Vishwakarma: official portal and NSDC guidelines describe support for traditional artisans and craftspeople aged 18 or above. Sources: https://pmvishwakarma.gov.in/ and https://nsdcindia.org/pmvishwakarma.html
- Telangana Aarogyasri: official Aarogyasri Health Care Trust portal. Source: https://aarogyasri.telangana.gov.in/
- Telangana KCR Kit: Hyderabad District Telangana government scheme page. Source: https://hyderabad.telangana.gov.in/scheme/kcr-kit/
- Telangana Rythu Bima: Telangana Rythu Bharosa/Rythu Bima official portal describes farmer group life insurance. Source: https://www.rythubharosa.telangana.gov.in/Default_Home.aspx
- AP NTR Bharosa Pension: official SSPensions portal lists pension categories and common eligibility. Source: https://sspensions.ap.gov.in/SSP/Home/About
- AP Thalliki Vandanam: Government of Andhra Pradesh scheme guidelines PDF. Source: https://gsws-nbm.ap.gov.in/NBM/images/GovtSchemesGos/Andhra%20Pradesh%20Thalliki%20Vandanam%20Scheme%20Guidelines.pdf
- AP Deepam 2.0: MeeSeva is the application service portal used for Andhra Pradesh citizen services. Source: https://ap.meeseva.gov.in/

## Data Entry Notes

- Some schemes use ration card or deprivation criteria instead of a simple income limit. For the app demo, approximate income limits are used so the rule engine can match users.
- Official scheme rules can change. Recheck links before final PPT or report submission.
